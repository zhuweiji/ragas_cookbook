Question: What is a Pod in Kubernetes?
Answer: A Pod in Kubernetes includes one or more containers.

Question: How can you balance network traffic across Pods?
Answer: You can create an external or internal network load balancer to balance network traffic across Pods.

Question: What is the role of the Kubernetes Vertical Pod Autoscaler?
Answer: The Kubernetes Vertical Pod Autoscaler scales Pods up or down vertically to meet demand.

Question: How can you restrict IP addresses that can be assigned to a service in Kubernetes?
Answer: You can restrict IP addresses that can be assigned to a service with `externalIPs`.

Question: What helps new users deploy a sample application in Kubernetes?
Answer: The topic "Deploy a sample application" helps new users deploy a sample application in Kubernetes.
